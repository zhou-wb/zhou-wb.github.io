Promote Excellence and Sustain Quality:

attract and deliver high-quality results, prioritizing integrity, content, presentation and experience.

Break down silos between disciplines and encourage collaboration to advance the theory and practice of computer graphics and interactive techniques.

Continuously review processes and results to support community satisfaction, using past experiences as opportunities for improvement.

Manage growth and evolution of programming and products to sustain competitiveness, deliver resources and drive innovation.
